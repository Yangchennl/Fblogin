EMAIL Detection Techniques
1. Content Analysis: Examines the email's body, subject, and attachments for suspicious keywords, phrases, or links commonly used in phishing (e.g., urgent requests for personal information).
2. URL Analysis: Checks links within emails for suspicious patterns, such as shortened URLs or mismatches between the link text and the actual destination URL.
3. Machine Learning: Trains models on large datasets of phishing and legitimate emails to identify patterns and classify future emails based on features like sender address, email content, and structure.
